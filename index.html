Why publish-first beats perfect-first in digital marketing

The biggest mistake early marketers make is waiting until everything is polished before going live. Search Console needs time to index. GA4 needs data to analyze. The earlier you publish, the earlier your clock starts — and SEO changes can take weeks to months to appear in search results.
